Why you should create an account

Many web pages allow you to enter comments without creating an account. We want you to create an account for a few reasons:

  • This way, nobody else can impersonate you in conversations.
  • We can let you edit your comments to correct mis-spellings and add information (although others will still be able to see the history of your comments, so don't think you're home free).
  • We do attempt a little social engineering, tracking your participation in the community and restricting access to people who want to be a part of it is easier if you have an account.

Regarding your email address:

Feel totally free to not give us your email address. If you enter it, we'll safeguard it as best we can, and only use it in a few situations:

  • If you forget your password.
  • If one of the people running the system notices something that needs clarification from you. This can be anything from "Hey, can you follow up on your comment on..." to "we noticed you discovered a bug...".
  • If you go to your User Information screen and tell us it's okay to publish your email in additional places, such as the NNTP syndication feed.
  • As a login to the news server, if you choose to use it.
  • The default will alwys be to safeguard your information, and you can always give it to us later if you decide to trust us with it.

(Huh, after all that I can't come up with too many good ideas as to why you would share your email address. No worries, your call.)
